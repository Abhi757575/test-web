Frequently Asked Questions

How can I submit a book for review?
Authors and publishers can submit books for review by filling out the contact form above and selecting "Partnership Opportunities." Please include details about your book including title, author, genre, publication date, and a brief synopsis. Our team will get back to you within 5-7 business days.
How can I join ReviewNet as a reviewer?
We're always looking for passionate readers to join our team! Fill out the contact form and select "Become a Reviewer." Please include information about your reading preferences, any writing experience, and why you want to join our team. Include a sample review (300-500 words) of a book you've recently read.
How long does it take to get a response?
We aim to respond to all inquiries within 48 hours during business days. For more complex requests or submissions, it may take up to a week. If you haven't heard back from us after a week, please check your spam folder or send a follow-up email.
Do you offer advertising opportunities on your website?
Yes, we offer various advertising options for publishers, authors, and book-related businesses. Please contact us through the form and select "Partnership Opportunities" to discuss advertising rates and placement options. We can create custom packages to suit different budgets and promotional needs.
